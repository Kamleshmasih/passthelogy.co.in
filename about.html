Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

PASS Theological Seminary was founded with a deep passion for the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Our goal is to equip, mentor, and prepare men and women for the ministry through rigorous biblical studies, spiritual formation, and practical leadership training.

Whether called to pastoral ministry, global missions, counseling, or academic service, we offer a rich environment where rigorous scholarship is beautifully balanced with vital spiritual maturity and active local church service.

Accreditation & Academic Integrity

PASS Theological Seminary is proud to be a fully accredited member institution of the Network for Theological Association (NTA Asia). NTA Asia is a global peer-based theological accrediting body dedicated to establishing and promoting the highest standards of academic excellence, continuous institutional quality assurance, and uncompromised evangelical integrity.

Our programs and institution align with NTA’s core theological quality criteria, conforming to internationally recognized academic frameworks (such as the ISO 21001 principles) and the global doctrinal baseline of the World Evangelical Alliance (WEA) Statement of Faith.

Curricular Rigor

Our program structures, learning outcomes, and course materials are regularly validated to meet high theological and academic benchmarks.

Faculty Credibility

All faculty members and academic mentors hold distinguished credentials and maintain ongoing professional development.

Ongoing Assurance

We actively participate in NTA's peer reviews, institutional self-assessments, and periodic audits to keep improving.
