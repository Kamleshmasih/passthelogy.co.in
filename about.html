About Our Seminary

Rooted in Scripture, Focused on Ministry, committed to Excellence.

Who We Are

Our Heritage & Commitment

PASS Theological Seminary was established to provide sound biblical and theological education to students called by God to serve in various ministry contexts. We believe in preparing students completely—mentally, spiritually, and practically—to address the complex challenges of the contemporary global church.

Through foundational theological disciplines, dynamic community life, and mentored ministry execution, we cultivate robust academic capacity alongside passionate Christian character.

Our Foundations

The core values guiding our institutional strategy and classroom learning environments.

Our Vision

To be an exceptional theological institution globally recognized for fostering deep biblical scholarship, authentic spiritual transformation, and bold missionary zeal within our student community.

Our Mission

To train, educate, and equip generations of faithful pastors, teachers, missionaries, and church leaders through an uncompromisingly biblical curriculum and focused mentorship structures.

Equipping faithful servants for gospel ministry, cross-cultural missions, and theological leadership.
