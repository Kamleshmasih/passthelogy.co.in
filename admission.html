How to Apply

We welcome applications from individuals who demonstrate a clear call to Christian service, spiritual maturity, and a commitment to academic discipline.

To ensure an organized review by our admissions board, please download the formal application prospectus, complete all required sections, and submit it along with your pastoral recommendation form.

Download Application & Prospectus (PDF)

General Requirements

Please prepare the following documents prior to submission:

  • Completed Application Form with passport-size photographs.
  • Official academic certificates and transcripts from previous institutions.
  • A written personal testimony detailing your Christian faith and ministry calling.
  • A formal recommendation letter from your local church pastor.
